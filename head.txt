The head tag doesn't render into the DOM, but is a crucial part of any html document.



The purpose of the head tag is to store external dependancies like stylesheets andscripts.
 Also, meta information like the title of the page, resposiveness, SEO.
 
<head>
 <title>Example Title</title>
 <meta name="author" content="Ed Woulfe">
 <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
 <link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="creating-circle.css">
 <script src="/example.js"></script>
</head>
